How to Use AttendBot

🚀 Getting Started

  • /startOpen the bot and register with your full name. Sets up your profile and shows the persistent keyboard.
  • /helpDisplay the welcome message with all available commands and the keyboard menu.

📋 Daily Attendance

  • ✅ Mark PresentTap the button (or type it) to mark yourself present for today. Works only on weekdays.
  • 🏖️ Take LeaveMark a leave day. Deducts from your monthly leave quota (4 per month).

📊 Stats & History

  • 📊 Check BalanceView your monthly attendance breakdown — present days, leaves taken, remaining leaves, and a visual calendar strip.
  • /statusSame as Check Balance — view your personal monthly stats via slash command.
  • /rename NameChange your display name on records. E.g. /rename John Doe

🔐 Admin Commands

  • 📋 Admin ReportGenerate a full team attendance summary for any month with per-employee stats.
  • 📥 Export CSVDownload a CSV spreadsheet of attendance. Supports: /export, /export last, /export May 2025
  • /whois nameLook up an employee's details by name or username.
  • /remove idArchive an employee. E.g. /remove 123456789
  • /archivedView list of all archived employees.
  • /restore idRestore an archived employee. E.g. /restore 123456789
  • /broadcast msgSend a message to all registered employees at once.
  • /admin passwordSelf-promote to admin access for evaluation. Use the configured admin password.
  • /holidaysList all registered public holidays for the current year.
  • /addholiday cmdAdd a holiday or range. E.g. /addholiday 2026-06-15 Eid
  • /removeholiday cmdRemove a holiday or range. E.g. /removeholiday 2026-06-15
Tip: The bot uses a persistent keyboard — just tap the buttons at the bottom of the chat. No need to memorize commands!
Note: Attendance can only be marked on weekdays (Mon–Fri). Weekend marking is disabled.
